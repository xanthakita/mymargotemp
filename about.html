Lori L. Lloyd Owner/Lash Artist

Lori is a wife to Andy of 37+ years, mother to 5 grown children, and grandmother to 8 gorgeous grandchildren! She wasn't always interested in the beauty business. For several years she was a midwife assistant and was a part of many many beautiful birth experiences! Feeling the need to make a career change she was looking for a something different. She learned the art by watching someone else demonstrate and then practiced on anyone who would let her! That was 7+ years ago and at that time classes were hard to find and typically far away with a high price tag! After practicing for several months she was able to move into a room at a local Doctor's office and starting taking clients. Lori's Lovely Lashes was born! She felt fortunate if she had 4 or 5 clients a month! After years of seeing a few clients every month, in the summer of 2016 the business really took off and she ask her daughter in law to join the business. By the end of that year it was obvious that she needed to move into a space of her own. It wasn't long before ANOTHER lash artist was added and now more than a year later we move into a bigger space!

Katelyn Lloyd Lash Artist

Katelyn learned the art of eyelash extensions while she was in school for Cosmetology. She learned the techniques outside of the Cosmetology school. She has been doing eyelash extensions off and on since 2010. In July of 2016 she joined the team at Lori's Lovely Lashes. She is known for her beautiful Volume sets. She has several very satisfied clients and they love her attention to details and charming personality.

Sylvia HudsonLash Artist

Sylvia has been a lash artist since the first part 2017 after completing her schooling for eyelash technicians. She joined the team at Lori's Lovely Lashes in June of 2017. Since then she has received several positive reviews! Clients love her warm personality and attention to detail. She specializes in Classic and Volume lash extensions.
