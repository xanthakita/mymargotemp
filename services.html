Description of Services

Classic Lash Services

Classic Lashes

One extension is applied to each of the natural lashes. Creates a beautiful natural look. We have many lengths, curls and thicknesses to achieve a customized look for each individual.

Classic Lash Fill In

Natural lashes shed every day, therefore the extensions need to be replaced every 2-4 weeks

Classic Mini Fill

The mini fill is designed to be an every week appointment OR done right before a special event and you want the lashes to look fresh!

3 week fill

If the loss is great and it has been three weeks since your last visit you MAY be charged the 3 week fill in price.

4 week fill

If the loss is great and it has been four weeks since your last visit you MAY be charged the 4 week fill in price.

Volume Lash Services

Volume Full Set

Russian volume sets are created by the lash artist taking very fine extensions and creating a fan. They are created using 3-8 very thin extensions. One fan is placed on one of the natural lashes, creating a very glamorous look!

Volume Fill In

Natural lashes shed every day, therefore the extensions need to be replaced every 2-4 weeks

Volume Mini Fill

The mini fill is designed to be an every week appointment OR done right before a special event when you want the lashes to look fresh!

3 week fill

If the loss is great and it has been three weeks since your last visit you MAY be charged the 3 week fill in price.

4 week fill

If the loss is great and it has been four weeks since your last visit you MAY be charged the 4 week fill in price.

Additional Lash Services

PreMade Volume Fans

Pre made fans come already fused together and are not built at the appointment. They do not wrap around the natural lash but rather lay on top. They create a beautiful full look.

Bottom Lashes

Sometimes clients want extensions applied to the bottom lashes. Not all lashes are covered.

Lash Removal

Gentle removal of the extensions without damage to the natural lash.

Hand or Foot treatment

Applying lotion filled gloves or footies to soothe and moisturize the hands and feet. Usually added to an eyelash appointment.

Tanning Services

Original

This organic solution has a special blend of aloe vera, walnut extract, and Vitamin C.

It is designed to rejuvenate and lock in moisture. It can be tailored to fit any skin type from fair to dark skin. Tans using this solution will last 7-10 days before significant fading is seen (with proper care). The Original has a processing time of 6-8 hours before showering.

Premium

This organic solution is fast-drying, meaning it dries on the body after sprayed because it is an alcohol based product. It has a special blend of vitamin C, caramel extract, and walnut extract.

This solution can be applied in a light-medium shade or medium-dark shade. Tans using this solution will last 5-7 days before significant fading is seen (with proper care). Premium has a processing time of 8-12 hours before showering.

Quickie

This organic solution has ultra fast dry times for someone who needs to shower in 2-4 hours. It is designed for all skin types and processing times depends on the desired shade. This solution was not intended to stay on longer than 4-5 hours. It has a special blend of vitamin C, wallet extract and caramel extract. The Quickie solution will last 5-7 days before significant fading is seen (with proper care).
